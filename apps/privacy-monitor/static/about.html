About Privacy Monitor

Privacy Monitor helps you know what type of information is being accessed by your apps and how frequently they are accessing it.

Our main mission is to give the access control to the you (user) so you can set the app-to-service access frequency limit to every installed app

We are not collecting any information from your phone and selling it to anyone.

So what we do?
We monitor installed apps and log the required information (like what app is accessing what type of service?, and when is it accessing?

Once logged into the database (which is inside your phone), we just alert you by comparing the limit what you set with the actual access frequency.

If you have any feedback or questions which can help us improve our app - please visit our Contact Us option.
