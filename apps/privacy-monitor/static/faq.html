FAQ

  1. How to uninstall the app?

    As this is a Certified App, you can uninstall it from Settings -> App Permissions --> Uninstall app


  2. How to set the custom access limit to services for any specific app?

    Click on Edit Access Frequency
    Select the app
    Just drag the range scroller to what ever number you want to set. (Note: This is access frequencey to that service per one hour).
